About ResumeAI

ResumeAI is an intelligent resume analysis platform that bridges the gap between job seekers and recruiters. Powered by advanced AI, it delivers instant ATS compatibility scoring, keyword optimization, and actionable feedback — helping candidates land interviews and helping recruiters find the right talent faster.

Our Mission

We believe hiring should be smarter, faster, and fairer. Our mission is to leverage AI to eliminate guesswork from the resume screening process — empowering students to present their best selves and enabling recruiters to make confident, data-driven decisions.
